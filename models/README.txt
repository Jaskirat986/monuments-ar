Place your 3D models here.

Recommended:
- GLB/GLTF for general web and Android AR (Scene Viewer).
- USDZ for iOS AR (Quick Look).

Example filenames used by the site:
- taj_mahal.glb, taj_mahal.usdz
- eiffel_tower.glb, eiffel_tower.usdz
- giza_pyramids.glb, giza_pyramids.usdz

You can rename files, but then update paths in ../app.js and ../index.html.
